projects

Personal and academic works

Digital art

Underwater City

In this project we had to represent the thematic "Urban mobility" and so I drew an underwater city in which we can see a kid riding a manta ray as its means of transport. This work was produced for the Digital Painting Technique course, from the Visual Arts and Tecnology degree on the Escola Superior de Educação de Lisboa University.

Foxie

Foxie is a character that described my personal representation. In this drawing I intended to capture some of my own personal features and likes.

Clio

Clio is a Pokémon I created for a contest that was being held on FNAC. This Pokémon is based on a clione, a sea slug, they are small colorful creatures that live under the sea, they can also be called sea angels due to these characteristics.

3D modeling

Character modeling

On this project, we were asked to create a character, in this case the Pokémon Charmander, from base on the Blender software. This was also my first modeling work and I'm pretty happy with the result. This work was produced for the Digital Representation course, from the Visual Arts and Tecnology degree on the Escola Superior de Educação de Lisboa University.

Coffee mug

This was one of my first projects on the AutoCAD software. We had to create an original mug for coffee and its plate. I came up with an art related mug, in which the handle was a paintbrush and the plate was a pallete with a cavity for the spoon. This work was produced for the Representation Tools and Techniques course, from the Visual Arts and Tecnology degree on the Escola Superior de Educação de Lisboa University.

Extreme Makeover

For this last project of the course, we had to do an extreme makeover of an already given 3D mesh on the Blender software. So I decided to transform it into a crystal dragon creature. We also had to add a background and a foreground object and for this I used the sky and a cloud. This work was produced for the Digital Representation course, from the Visual Arts and Tecnology degree on the Escola Superior de Educação de Lisboa University.

Graphic Design

Branding

On this project we had to create a logo from scratch, it could be a branding or a rebranding. I chose to create my own japanese collection figure store logo and for this I named it "Kokoro", which means Heart in japanese and gave it a red circle to resemble not only the heart color but also the japanese flag. This work was produced for the Graphic Design course, from the Visual Arts and Tecnology degree on the Escola Superior de Educação de Lisboa University.

Banco de Benfica logo

For this assignement, we had to also create a logo that would represent the brand "Banco de Benfica", a brand created for our benches that were produced on the FabLab of our University. I designed a B shape that resembled a bench and at the same time the two initials of the words "Banco" and "Benfica". This work was produced for the Graphic Design course, from the Visual Arts and Tecnology degree on the Escola Superior de Educação de Lisboa University.
